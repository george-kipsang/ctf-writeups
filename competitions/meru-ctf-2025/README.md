## Meru University CTF 2025

**Date Held:** June 14, 2025

**Organizer:** Meru University

**Event Type:** Flash CTF (Jeopardy Style)

**Duration:** 2 hours
## Event Overview

The Meru University CTF 2025 was a fast-paced, jeopardy-style cybersecurity challenge that tested skills across multiple categories. Participants had two hours to solve a variety of tasks ranging from cryptography and forensics to web exploitation and reverse engineering.

This repository contains detailed writeups and solutions for some of the challenges encountered during the event.
